About Us

We are a family-owned company dedicated to the production, packaging, and export of cherries and other fruits. We are located in Chile’s fruit heartland, in the Maule Region. For more than 15 years, we have been working in this industry to deliver the highest quality fruits to the most demanding markets worldwide. Together with our collaborators, growers, and suppliers, we are committed to producing our fruits in an environmentally friendly way, working in a clean and healthy environment to achieve the best possible quality.
